Learn About Our Efficient Workflow

  • Receiving the Request

    Fill out the form with your details and submit it to our managers for processing.

  • Consultation

    Once your request is processed, we will contact you to discuss your needs and preferences. We also provide a full technical consultation.

  • On-Site Visit

    At the scheduled time, we visit the site to align on key technical solutions and discuss the project specifications.

  • Scheduling & Estimation

    At this stage, we prepare a commercial proposal and align on the project schedule, including design, equipment procurement, and installation timelines.

  • Contract Signing

    We sign a contract with you and begin the design phase, including project engineering and approval.

  • Delivery & Installation

    At this stage, all materials are delivered to the site, and the installation work begins.
